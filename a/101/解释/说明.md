## 本版目标

新增 dry-run access guard：

```text
所有请求响应头增加 x-orderops-access-*
GET /api/v1/security/access-guard-readiness
GET /api/v1/security/access-guard-readiness?format=markdown
```

响应头包含：

```text
x-orderops-access-guard-mode
x-orderops-access-policy-id
x-orderops-access-route-group
x-orderops-access-required-role
x-orderops-access-matched-roles
x-orderops-access-would-deny
x-orderops-access-reason
```

本版只 dry-run，不拒绝请求。
